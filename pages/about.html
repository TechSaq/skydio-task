About Us

WHAT WE DO

The sky is an indispensable source of vital business data and us at Aeronics Consultoria Pvt Ltd provide best in class Drone based services for technical, Academic & Entertainment Industry. We offer a wide range of drone services for our customers to increase the value of their assets. We lead our business with the manufacturing of high-quality drones that are capable of acquiring high precision data information with the adaptive flight time. We offer a variety of services which include aerial videography, bathymetric survey, agriculture spraying, soil survey & investigation, quarry survey, and many more. You can check all our services at the “our services” section.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide fast, reliable, and affordable data from the air so our customers can make well-precise, real-time decisions of their most critical assets. After a very long time, the sky is now available for commercialization for the young generation of business-minded people and our mission is to seize this opportunity at an elevated level.

OUR FOUNDER

Alim Khan is the founder and CEO of Aeronics Consultoria Pvt Ltd and holds the degree of Aerospace Engineering. He initially became a drone enthusiast in the second year of college when he planned to map the college premises. Alim has been always dedicated to the passion he follows, as a result; He has been awarded the NASA system Engineer title in 2013 and later on he secured AIR 316 rank in GATE 2014. He holds the ability to focus on his vision and communicate that vision to his partners and stakeholders. Under Alim’s vision and leadership, ACP has become the trusted, go-to drone platform for a wide range of drone services that have successfully satisfied a plethora number of customers.

OUR HEADQUARTER

Headquartered in Lucknow, our branches are outspread at Delhi, Kolkata, and Banglore. ACP is home to perspicacious team members who are passionate about drones, solving challenging problems, and providing extremely precise data results.
